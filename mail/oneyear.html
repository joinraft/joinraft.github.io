One year ago today, on January 26 2015, we launched Raft in Sweden. It's been an amazing year and we want to thank all of you using Raft!


Here's are some things we've learned about planning

  • It helps to make it fun to plan. Add GIFs and pictures, and even boring stuff becomes fun to plan. 
  • Share events. Telling your friends about your plans makes it more likely you'll reach your goals. 
  • Routines are hard. It often takes 5-7 days for new users to get the routine to use Raft. So hang in there. 

What will you do this year? Start by adding it to Raft.
