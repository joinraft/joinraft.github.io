Today we celebrate 1 year!

One year ago, on January 26 2015, we launched Raft in Sweden. Since then we have found our way to 89 countries around the world. It's been an amazing year and we hope that Raft has helped many of you.

Here's what we've learned about making planning fun:

1. An image is worth 1000 words. A GIF millions.
Needless to say, we made it possible to add GIFs and pictures, and voila, even boring stuff becomes fun to plan.

2. Sharing plans makes them stick
Telling your friends about your plans, even outside Raft, makes it more likely you'll reach your goals. Now you can, just tap the "Share" button :)

3. Having things to look forward to things makes us happier.
With this in mind we build a beautiful view where all the things you have planned is listed with big beautiful images.

BUT most importantly: Planning is better together
Raft is best when used with others. So before you head back into Raft, tell your friends to join you in planning 2016 together.
