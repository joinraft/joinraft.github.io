We want to make it as easy as possible for you to access Raft and see what you’ve got planned.

Got an iPad? Just download Raft and connect with your Facebook account to view your plans on the iPad too.

Or are you leaving on a jetplane? What you’ve added in Raft is available in flight mode. But the chat and feed won’t refresh without an internet connection.

Or have you lost your iPhone? Or maybe upgrading a new one? Let’s hope for the latter. Anyhow. You’ve guessed it - download Raft and you’ll find your events, friends and chats just as you left them.
