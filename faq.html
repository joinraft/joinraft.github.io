Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can browse a collection on the most common questions we get about Raft. If you can't find an answer to your question just shoot an email to hello@joinraft.com or ask us on Twitter.

Why should I use Raft?

Because Raft is the best way to share your calendar with your friends and family. To get more done, avoid misunderstandings, see what your friends got planned, and do more fun together. That’s why :)

We believe that it is a good idea to plan things that you want to do, big and small, but traditional calendars are so boring it takes the fun out of planning. Especially if you’re planning something fun. Raft is beautifully designed, easy to use and since you’re supposed to share some of your plans with your friends, it is also much more fun to use than any other calendar out there.

Do I have to share calendar with my friends?

No you don’t have to share anything if you don’t want to. A calendar could be a pretty private space, and few of our users share everything. But that’s the beauty of Raft. You can set sharing settings on each event. For the plans you don’t want to share - well don’t share them. And for the things you want your partner or friends to know about, share and let them know.

How does sharing calendar with my partner work?

If you have a special someone (congratulations) and you’re both using Raft you’re all set to create a partner connection. That is pretty smart and adds an extra dimension to Raft. What you share with your partner is only visible to the two of you.

Sharing calendar with you partner, BFF or significant other is a well liked feature by many of our users, since it makes it much easier to plan everyday life.

Can I share events from Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook too?

Yes if you want to you can import other calendars to Raft. You decide what calendars to show, and please note that other calendars will not be made visible to any other person except yourself. Not until you manually copy an imported event to Raft can you decide if you want to share the event with your partner and/or friends.

So you’re not running the risk of accidentally sharing information from another calendar to your followers on Raft, it is up to you to decide on each event if you want to copy it or not.

I’m not allowed to share my work calendar, can I still use Raft.

Sure! Simply don’t allow Raft to read your other calendars. And if you do allow it, keep in mind that you’re not sharing this information with anybody (we don’t know about it). It is only visible in your Raft.

What is the difference between inviting someone and sharing an event with someone?

Yes this is a bit tricky. You could say that you invite a person to an event if you want them to join, but you share an event with someone if you just want them to know what you’re up to. If you invite someone they get an invitation, and you can send messages to each other that are only visible to the people invited to the event.

This is something that differ Raft from other calendars so it might take some time to understand. The best way is to just play around in Raft for 5 minutes and we’re sure you’ll get the hang of it.

What is “The Feed”

A: Yeah, this is a unique feature with Raft too. The Feed is where you can discover what you’re friends have shared with you. You can like and comment on their plans, and ask if you can join. And it works both ways of course. What you share with your followers, is seen in their feeds.

This sounds awesome, does Raft cost anything?

No, Raft is free to use.

Great, but how do you make money?

We don’t. Not yet at least. But in the future we might invite brands and companies to Raft. And if they want to publish events and get followers they will be charged for this.

Why are you using Facebook to log-in?

Because it is really fast and easy way to get started. You don’t have to create an account and enter you email and keep track of another password.

Will you publish stuff on my Facebook wall?

No we won’t. The information we get when you log in with your Facebook-account is your email (optional), your profile picture, your name and a list of your friends that are already using Raft. We understand that it might feel scary to log in with Facebook, but we won’t publish anything on your wall.

I don’t have a Facebook account, does that mean I can’t use Raft?

A: No you can’t. At least not at the moment. But we’ll add the possibility to log in with your phone number soon. And if you’re really eager to use Raft, and don’t use Facebook, mail us at hello@joinraft.com and we’ll hook you up :)

I have an iPad, does Raft do iPads?

It does! If you log in with the same user as on your iPhone, all the information you’ve added on your iPhone will be available on the iPad. And you know, it works the other way around too.

Is Raft available on Android?

No not yet sorry. We’ll build an Android app eventually, but it is much easier for us to focus on the iPhone app at the moment. But sign up, and we’ll notify you when the Android app is on its way.

I have some suggestions on how to improve Raft. How do I get in contact with you?

Awesome! Talk to us on Facebook, or send an email to anders@joinraft.com

Who are the team behind Raft?

A: We’re three friends from Stockholm in northern Europe. Our names are Anders, Erik and Fredrik. We’re born in the 80s and have been working on Raft since 2013.

Why are you building Raft?

Because we want people to be happier and enjoy life more. Today, too much focus is on work and making a career, and it’s stressful. We think we all feel better if we spend time with loved ones and do more of what makes us happy. All other things doesn't really matter that much in the long run. At least that’s what we think.

So we’ve built Raft to make it easy and fun to share plans and calendars and to encourage spontaneous meetings and get togethers with friends and family. We hope you like Raft :)
