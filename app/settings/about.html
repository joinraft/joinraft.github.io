Hello and thanks for using Raft!

Raft is about finding out what makes you happy, and do more of it. Because we believe in having fun. And sure, we can’t know what makes you smile.. but we hope that making it easy and fun to plan things with the people you care about is a good first step. That’s why we’ve created Raft.

Raft is made in Stockholm in Northern Europe. We like it here. Ping us if you're in town and want to meet. And if you have any ideas on how we could improve Raft, we'd love to hear them. Send an email to anders@joinraft.com or get in contact on Twitter @joinraft
